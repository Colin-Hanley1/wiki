# Guang Hao Low

Researcher in quantum computing, affiliated with [[MIT]] at the time of the Bayesian inference work (2014). Co-author of [[Quantum Inference on Bayesian Networks]] with [[Theodore J. Yoder]] and [[Isaac L. Chuang]].

Known for work on quantum algorithms, including Hamiltonian simulation and quantum signal processing.
